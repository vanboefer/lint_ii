LiNT-II: readability assessment for Dutch

Jenia Kim

LiNT-II analyzes Dutch text readability using four linguistic features: word frequency, concrete vs abstract nouns, syntactic dependency length, and content words per clause. Each sentence receives a difficulty score from 1 (easy) to 4 (difficult).

Try the interactive visualizationThe passage is from "Een nagelaten bekentenis" (1894) by Marcellus Emants below to explore sentence-level analysis. Hover over words to see their linguistic features, or click Î£ to view document statistics.
